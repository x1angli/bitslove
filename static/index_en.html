Who Are We

BITSLOVE is world's first non-profitable organization dedicated to provide speed fundraising platforms based on Blockchain for worldwide charity organizations. On this funding platform, charity and non-profitable organizations are enabled to optimize the various exclusive benefits of Blockchain as it is secure, swift, convenient and transparent in instantly raising funds all over the world. This is unprecedented and revolutionary for charity causes as well.

BITSLOVE provides platform solution for every and any charity or non-profitable organization, allowing them to set up their own page in the shortest amount of time to fund from donators from every corner of the world using Blockchain (including but not limited to Bitcoin, BitShares, BitUSD, etc.), and donate the raised fund to beneficiaries no mather where they are, through the addresses and QR-codes provided by the platform.

What's Blockchain?

With the first fully implemented Blockchain, Bitcoin as an example, Blockchain, cored with mathematical algorithm, purely based on the Internet. The transmission of Blockchain almost costs zero and it can also be conveniently converted to the local currencies of many countries with its legal status already acknowledged in China, the US and many European countries.

More Global Organizations Start to Accept Blockchain

An increasing number of charity organizations start to accept Bitcoin donation. Just in the summer this year, Wikipedia also joined the rank being the largest non-profitable organizations that accept Bitcoin so far. BitGive, on the other hand has recently become the first Bitcoin charity organization to have acquired tax exemption status.

United Way Worldwide Accepts Blockchain

In September 2014, United Way Worldwide, one of the largest non-profitable organizations in the world, declares that it accepts donation through Coinbase’s service. The organization has more than 2.8 million volunteers, 9.7 million donors, exceeding 5 billion USD a year of annual fundraising. Its partners include Generic Electric, Exxon Mobile and NFL.

Blockchain Offers More Transparency

Because of the very fact that Blockchain is decentralized and without an issuer, instead, through a technology called public ledger, it guarantees that no one would be able to tamper with the account book.
Every computer in the Bitcoin network has the same set of data of the account book. The system would compare whether every computer has the identical account book in order to secure data. Unless more than 51% of computers in the network are controlled, any attempt to tamper with the data is invalid, because every transaction happens in the network is published in the entire global network and is open to inquiry and thus individual manipulation is absolutely ruled out. Everyone could verify transactions through various third party websites, so the record of Blockchain donation is completely public and transparent.

Charity Program

April 2nd , 2011, Mr. Deng Fei, joined by 500 reporters in China and more than 10 mainstream media, partnering with China Social Welfare Foundation initiated a public fund raising project for free lunches, proposing a daily donation of 3 yuan to help poor kids with free lunches. Since its official kick-start in April 2011 until July 2014, the program raised more than 9.9 million RMB, put lunch on the table for up to 402 schools for hungry kids. Furthermore, the program made huge impact on the State’s Council of Nutrition Improvement Program for students under compulsory education, altering millions of children’s chances to better nutrition on a huge scale.
